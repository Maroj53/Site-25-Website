Who are we?

Site 25 is a Roleplay SCP Secret Laboratory server, based on the SCP Wiki. Whole site is build around departments and roles. Under the command of the O5 Council,

Departments

  • The Overseer Council - A group of 13 high-ranking officials with ultimate control over the Foundation.
  • Ethics Committee - An independent board responsible for weighing the moral costs of the Foundation's containment.
  • O4 Council - A collective of every Site Director, Deparment Head and other significant personnel.
  • Internal Security Department - A secret police force responsible for filtering traitors as well as operational and information security risks among the Foundation's ranks.
  • Intelligence Agency - Conducts espionage on non-Foundation groups. Responsible for all undercover Foundation agents.
  • Security Department - Responsible for ensuring the security of Foundation facilities and the safety of personnel.
  • Research Department - Manages all research procedures and anomalies on site. Tests on D Class personnel
  • Medical Department - Provides medical support to Foundation operatives.
  • Technical Department - Develops Foundation technology and structures. Takes care of Foundation containments

Departments

  • Alpha-1 [ The Red Right Hand ] - Personal Task Force of the O5 Council.
  • Omega-1 [ Law's Left Hand ] - Personal Task Force of the Ethics Committee
  • Epsilon-11 [ Nine-Tailed Fox ] - Task Force specialising in securing facilities suffering from a Containment Breach.
  • Nu-7 [ Hammer Down ] - A full military battalion composed of thousands of soldiers and several military vehicles.
  • Beta-7 [ Maz Hatters ] - Foundation`s HAZMAT Team.
